We're thrilled to have you, . Here's a quick guide to get you started.

Step 1: Set Up Your Profile

Your journey begins with you! Click on your avatar in the bottom-left corner to open the profile menu. From there you can Edit Profile or change your Settings.

Step 2: Jump Into a Game

Ready for action? The Dashboard menu on the left has a Play Now button. This is your gateway to finding and joining game rooms.

Step 3: Connect With Friends

Gaming is better together. Use the Friends and Chat links in the sidebar to add new friends, manage your friend list, and start conversations.

Step 4: Explore Your Dashboard

This is your command center. Everything you need is in the sidebar on the left. Stay on top of alerts with Notifications and always know who's online.
